Privacy Policy for Task Tracker Pro Chrome Extension

1. Information Collection and Use
   Task Tracker Pro does not collect any personal information. All task data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

2. Data Storage
   Your tasks and time tracking data are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

3. Third-party Services
   This extension does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.

   We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
